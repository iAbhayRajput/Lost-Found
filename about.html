About SRM

SRM University Delhi-NCR, Sonepat, Haryana (SRM Education and Research Institute) has been established under the Haryana Private University Act, 2006 as amended by Act No. 8 of 2013. SRM University Delhi-NCR, Sonepat, Haryana is a leading world-class university that creates and disseminates knowledge upholding the highest standards of instruction in Engineering and Technology, Science & Humanities, Medicine & Health Sciences, and Law & Management Studies. It carries a n ideology rooted in optimism and a hunger to pursue progress. Along with academic excellence and skills, the university curriculum imparts integrity and social sensitivity to mold university graduates who may be best suited to serve the nation and the world.
